I love micro watch brands. They’re small, scrappy, and driven by people who actually care about design, craftsmanship, and storytelling. But if you’re running one, you already know it’s a ton of work. You’re designing, managing suppliers, handling customer questions, shipping orders, and trying to keep the brand alive all at once. 

The tech side? It often gets pushed to the bottom of the list. That’s where I come in.  

---

## The Common Challenges Micro Brands Face

Most of the founders I talk to run into the same problems:

- Setting up (and actually maintaining) an online store  
- Payments, shipping, and international customers  
- Getting marketing tools working (email lists, reviews, preorders)  
- Making sure the site doesn’t break when a new drop goes live  
- Just not having enough time or bandwidth to deal with all of it  

---
